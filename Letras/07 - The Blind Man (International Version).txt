[Título: The Blind Man (International Version)]

[Introduction]

The Blind Man.

The legendary sniper.

The self-proclaimed MVP.

The only man capable of missing every shot…

and still claiming the kill.

[Verse 1]

Every night he joins the lobby.

Every night he makes the same speech.

“I got him.”

“I broke his armor.”

“I knocked him.”

Meanwhile…

Trip gets the kill.

Biel gets the kill.

Juninho gets the kill.

And somehow…

The Blind Man takes the credit.

[Pre-Chorus]

The scoreboard says one thing.

His mouth says another.

Nobody knows where reality ends.

And where his imagination begins.

[Chorus]

The Blind Man!

King of confusion!

Hero of delusion!

Lord of self-congratulation!

The Blind Man!

Missing every bullet!

Claiming every kill!

Living in his own simulation!

[Verse 2]

He says he’s a sniper.

A deadly hunter.

A long-range assassin.

But after twenty shots…

The enemy is still alive.

The wall is not.

The tree is not.

The building is not.

The entire neighborhood is not.

[Verse 3]

One day he fired so many shots

at the same target

that he accidentally drew

the complete silhouette of the enemy

on the wall behind him.

A perfect outline.

A masterpiece.

Everything except actual damage.

[Chorus]

The Blind Man!

King of confusion!

Hero of delusion!

Lord of self-congratulation!

The Blind Man!

Missing every bullet!

Claiming every kill!

Living in his own simulation!

[Verse 4]

Then came Biel.

The apprentice.

The student.

The intern.

The young recruit.

The one who was supposed to learn.

Instead…

He evolved.

He adapted.

He started getting more kills.

More wins.

More highlights.

And suddenly…

The apprentice surpassed the master.

[Bridge]

A tragedy.

A betrayal.

A historical event.

The student became the teacher.

And The Blind Man never recovered.

[Verse 5]

His computer should be in a museum.

Call of Duty runs on minimum settings.

Minimum textures.

Minimum shadows.

Minimum dignity.

The game looks like

Minecraft fighting Tibia

inside a microwave.

Sometimes we’re not even sure

he’s playing the same game.

[Breakdown]

“Enemy on the roof!”

“What roof?”

“I can’t see the roof!”

“There is no roof!”

“Everything is squares!”

[Final Chorus]

The Blind Man!

Master of excuses!

Victim of his hardware!

Champion of alternate realities!

The Blind Man!

Missing every bullet!

Claiming every kill!

Still searching for the perfect sniper shot!

[Outro]

Trip got the kill.

Biel got the kill.

Juninho got the kill.

The scoreboard got the kill.

Everybody got the kill.

Except…

The Blind Man.
